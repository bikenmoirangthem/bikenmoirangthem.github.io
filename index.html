Moirangthem Biken Singh

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Delhi Technological University (DTU), India. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi, India, under the supervision of Dr. Ajay Pratap. Previously, I completed my M.Tech degree under the supervision of Dr. Gyanendra Kumar Verma at the National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra, India, in 2021, and my B.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering under the guidance of Dr. Chingakham Neeta Devi at the National Institute of Technology, Manipur, India, in 2018. My research interests include IoT, Mobile Computing, AI/ML, and Federated Learning in Smart Healthcare.
